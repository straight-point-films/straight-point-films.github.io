About Us

With Gareth growing up in the Welsh countryside and Shane on the outskirts of London we first met at university back in 2011. Quickly finding out we had complementing skills and a love of film, we established our working relationship. Both wanting to hone our craft we went on separate paths into our expertises of editing and sound. Then coming back together in 2018 to start Straight Point Films.


We recently completed a BFI funded feature length documentary on the UK oldest performing drag queen, to be released at London BFI Film Festival.
